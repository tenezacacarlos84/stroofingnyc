Professional Chimney Services in NYC

A properly functioning chimney is essential for the safety of your home and family. Chimneys are vulnerable to damage from weather, age, and wear, which can lead to leaks, structural problems, and even fire hazards. From minor flashing repairs to complete chimney rebuilds, our experienced team has the expertise to handle all your chimney needs. We also install chimney caps, perform waterproofing, and provide regular inspections to keep your chimney operating safely and efficiently.

Chimney Flashing

Install or repair flashing where your chimney meets the roof. Stop leaks and protect your home from water damage.

Chimney Caps

Install caps to keep rain, animals, and debris out of your chimney. Prevent costly damage and improve safety.

Chimney Repair

Fix cracked brick, damaged mortar, spalling, and other issues. Restore your chimney's integrity.

Chimney Rebuild

Complete or partial chimney rebuilds for severely damaged structures. New brick, blocks, or stone.

Common Chimney Problems We Fix

Chimney Leaks

Water entering through damaged flashing, cracks, or missing caps causing interior damage

Damaged Flashing

Rusted, cracked, or improperly installed flashing leading to roof leaks

Spalling Bricks

Bricks crumbling or flaking due to freeze-thaw cycles and water penetration

Missing or Damaged Caps

No cap or damaged cap allowing water, animals, and debris into the chimney

Damaged Mortar

Deteriorating mortar joints allowing water infiltration and reducing structural integrity

White Staining (Efflorescence)

Mineral deposits indicating water penetration and potential brick damage

Why Chimney Maintenance Matters

  • Prevent Water Damage - Chimney leaks can cause extensive damage to walls, ceilings, and insulation
  • Fire Safety - Creosote buildup and damaged chimneys can create fire hazards
  • Structural Integrity - Damaged chimneys can compromise your home's structure
  • Animal Prevention - Uncapped chimneys invite animals seeking shelter
  • Cost Savings - Timely repairs prevent more expensive problems down the road
