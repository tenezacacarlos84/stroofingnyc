Signs You Need Roof Repair

Water Stains on Ceilings

Brown or yellow stains indicate a leak that needs immediate attention

Missing or Damaged Shingles

Cracked, curling, or missing shingles compromise your roof's integrity

Granules in Gutters

Excessive granules from shingles indicate advanced wear and possible failure

Sagging Roof Areas

Sagging can indicate structural damage or trapped moisture

Higher Energy Bills

Damaged roofing reduces insulation efficiency, increasing heating/cooling costs

Daylight Through Roof

Light visible through roof boards means serious damage and potential leaks

Repair vs. Replace: When to Fix vs. Replace

Not every roof problem requires a full replacement. Our honest assessment helps you make the right decision:

  • Repair is usually best when: Damage is localized, roof is relatively new (under 15 years), or the issue is isolated (a few shingles, minor flashing)
  • Replacement may be needed when: Roof is over 20 years old, widespread damage exists, multiple leaks are present, or repairs would cost more than 30% of replacement

We always provide an honest recommendation based on your specific situation and budget.
