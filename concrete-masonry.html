Frequently Asked Questions

Concrete costs vary based on the scope of work, size of the area, and any custom finishes. We provide free estimates with detailed pricing so you know exactly what to expect.

Concrete typically takes 24-48 hours to set enough to walk on and 7 days to reach full strength. We'll advise on proper care during the curing process.

Brick pointing (also called repointing) is the process of removing deteriorated mortar from between bricks and replacing it with fresh mortar. This prevents water infiltration and extends the life of your brickwork.

Properly done pointing can last 20-30 years or more. However, factors like weather exposure, brick quality, and original installation quality can affect lifespan. We recommend periodic inspections to catch problems early.
