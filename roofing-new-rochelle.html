How much does a new roof cost in New Rochelle?

How does salt air affect my roof in New Rochelle?

Salt air accelerates corrosion of metal components and can degrade roofing materials faster. We recommend marine-grade materials, proper coatings, and regular maintenance to extend roof life.

Do you work on Tudor-style homes in New Rochelle?

What roofing materials work best near the coast?

We recommend architectural shingles with algae resistance, metal roofing with marine-grade coatings, and properly sealed flat roof systems. We'll help you choose the best option for your coastal property.
