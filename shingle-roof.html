Quality Shingle Roofs That Last

Asphalt shingles remain the most popular roofing choice for residential homes in the United States, and for good reason. They offer an excellent balance of durability, aesthetics, and affordability. At ST Roofing & Construction, we specialize in professional shingle roof installation that protects your home for decades.

Whether you're building a new home or replacing an aging roof, our experienced team will guide you through the selection process to find the perfect shingle style and color to complement your home's architecture. We work with top-quality shingle manufacturers to ensure your new roof provides lasting protection and enhances your home's curb appeal.

Shingle Types We Install

  • 3-Tab Shingles - Economical option with classic look
  • Architectural Shingles - Dimensional look with enhanced durability
  • Premium/Luxury Shingles - Premium aesthetics with maximum protection
  • Designer Shingles - Unique styles to match any home

Benefits of Shingle Roofing

Affordable

Excellent value for your investment with competitive pricing on quality materials.

Variety

Wide range of colors, styles, and textures to match any home aesthetic.

Durable

Modern shingles offer excellent resistance to wind, rain, and UV rays.

Easy Repairs

Individual damaged shingles can be replaced without redoing the entire roof.

Fire Resistant

Class A fire rating available for added peace of mind and insurance benefits.

Energy Efficient

Reflective shingle options can help reduce cooling costs in summer.
