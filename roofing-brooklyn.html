Frequently Asked Questions - Brooklyn Roofing

How much does a new roof cost in Brooklyn?

Costs vary based on size, materials, and roof type. For Brooklyn properties, flat roof replacements range from $8,000-$20,000, brownstone roofings from $15,000-$35,000. We provide free estimates.

What is the best flat roof material for Brooklyn?

EPDM (rubber) is the most common and cost-effective option for Brooklyn's climate. Torch-down modified bitumen and TPO are also excellent choices depending on your building's specific needs.

Do you work on historic brownstones in Brooklyn?

Yes! We have extensive experience with Brooklyn brownstones. We understand the importance of preserving architectural character while providing modern weather protection.

How do I know if my flat roof needs replacement?

Signs include persistent leaks, ponding water, cracked or blistered membranes, and visible wear around flashings. Our free inspection will assess your roof's condition and recommend the best solution.
