Expert Skylight Installation

Skylights transform dark rooms into bright, inviting spaces filled with natural light. At ST Roofing & Construction, we specialize in professional skylight installation with expert flashing techniques that ensure a completely leak-free result on shingle roofs.

Whether you want to brighten a bathroom, kitchen, hallway, or attic space, we handle the entire process — from cutting the roof opening to installing proper flashing that integrates seamlessly with your shingle roof. Our installations in NJ and NYC have stood up to the toughest Northeast weather conditions.

Our Skylight Services

  • New Skylight Installation — Complete installation on shingle and flat roofs
  • Skylight Replacement — Upgrade old, foggy, or leaking skylights
  • Flashing & Waterproofing — Expert flashing integration with existing roofing
  • Leak Repair — Fix skylights that leak during rain or snow
  • Fixed Skylights — Non-opening units for maximum light
  • Venting Skylights — Opening units for ventilation and light

Benefits of Skylights

Natural Light

Skylights provide up to 30% more light than vertical windows of the same size.

Energy Savings

Reduce dependence on electric lighting during daytime hours, lowering energy bills.

Ventilation

Venting skylights release hot air and moisture, improving indoor air quality.

Health Benefits

Natural light boosts mood, vitamin D production, and overall well-being.

Home Value

Skylights are a desirable feature that increases property value and buyer interest.

Privacy

Get abundant light without sacrificing privacy — perfect for bathrooms and bedrooms.

Our Installation Process

Proper skylight installation is critical to prevent leaks. Our process ensures watertight results every time.

1

Roof Assessment

We evaluate your roof structure, determine the optimal skylight placement, and check for obstructions.

2

Opening & Framing

We cut the roof opening precisely and build a proper curb frame to support the skylight unit.

3

Flashing Installation

Step flashing and counter-flashing are carefully woven into the existing shingles for a watertight seal.

4

Skylight & Finishing

The skylight is mounted, sealed, and surrounding shingles are reinstalled for a clean, professional look.

Why Professional Installation Matters

The number one concern with skylights is leaking — and 90% of skylight leaks are caused by improper installation, not the skylight itself. That's why choosing an experienced roofing contractor for skylight work is critical.

At ST Roofing & Construction, our team understands the intricate relationship between skylights and roofing systems. We integrate the skylight flashing directly into your shingle roof following manufacturer specifications and roofing best practices.

Our Flashing Expertise

  • Step flashing woven into each shingle course
  • Ice and water shield membrane underlayment
  • Proper head, sill, and side flashing details
  • Counter-flashing for complete weather protection
  • Quality sealants rated for roofing applications

Leak-Free Guarantee

Professional flashing ensures your skylight stays dry through every storm.
