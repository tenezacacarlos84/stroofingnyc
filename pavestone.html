Pavestone & Masonry

Why Choose Pavestone?

Premium Aesthetics

Pavers come in dozens of colors, patterns, and textures to match any home style.

Extremely Durable

Pavers withstand freeze-thaw cycles, heavy traffic, and extreme weather without cracking.

Easy Maintenance

Individual pavers can be replaced if damaged — no need to redo the entire surface.

Proper Drainage

Joint sand between pavers allows water to drain naturally, preventing pooling.

Lighting Options

Embedded LED floor lights add safety and create stunning nighttime ambiance.

Adds Home Value

Quality hardscaping significantly increases your property's curb appeal and resale value.

Our Installation Process

Every pavestone project follows our proven 5-step process to ensure a level, long-lasting result.

1

Site Assessment

We evaluate your space, discuss design options, and plan the layout with proper grading for drainage.

2

Base Preparation

Excavation, compacted gravel base, and landscape fabric create a solid, stable foundation.

3

Screeding

A precisely leveled layer of sand is screeded to ensure a perfectly flat surface for paver placement.

4

Paver Installation

Pavers are laid in your chosen pattern, cut to fit edges, and any embedded lighting is wired.

5

Finishing & Compaction

Polymeric sand is swept into joints, edges are secured, and the surface is compacted for a solid finish.
