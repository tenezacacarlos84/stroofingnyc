Building Trust One Roof at a Time

ST Roofing & Construction LLC (also operating as ST General Waterproofing LLC) is a family-owned and operated roofing company proudly serving the New York City metropolitan area since 2020. With over 15 years of combined experience in the industry, we've built our reputation on quality workmanship, honest communication, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our founder, Carlos Tenezaca, brings deep expertise in all aspects of residential and commercial roofing. From simple repairs to complex installations, Carlos and his team approach every project with the same level of care and attention to detail. We believe that every customer deserves a roof that not only looks great but provides lasting protection for their home or business.

As a bilingual company, we serve both English and Spanish-speaking customers throughout our service area. We understand that roofing projects can be stressful, which is why we prioritize clear communication, transparent pricing, and respect for your property throughout the entire process.
