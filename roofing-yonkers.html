How much does a new roof cost in Yonkers?

How do you prevent ice dams on my Yonkers roof?

We address ice dams through proper insulation, ventilation, and optionally heated gutter cables. This keeps your roof surface cold and prevents snow from melting and ref-freezing at the gutters.

Do you work on historic Victorian homes in Yonkers?

Yes! We have extensive experience with Victorian-era homes in Yonkers. We understand the unique requirements of historic preservation while providing durable, modern roofing solutions.

What roofing materials work best in Yonkers?

We recommend architectural shingles for most homes, with proper underlayment for winter durability. For flat sections, torch-down or EPDM membranes provide excellent waterproofing for Hudson Valley weather.
