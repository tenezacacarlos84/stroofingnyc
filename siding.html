Expert Siding Services in NYC

Siding is one of the most important elements of your home's exterior. It protects your walls from the elements, adds insulation, and dramatically affects your home's curb appeal. ST Roofing & Construction LLC provides professional siding installation and replacement services throughout the NYC metro area.

Whether you're building a new home or replacing old, damaged siding, our experienced team will help you choose the best materials for your style, budget, and performance needs. We install all types of siding including vinyl, fiber cement, and wood, ensuring a beautiful, long-lasting result.

Vinyl Siding

Most popular choice. Affordable, low-maintenance, available in countless colors and styles. Won't rot or need painting.

Fiber Cement

Premium durability with the look of wood. Fire-resistant, pest-resistant, and warranty-backed up to 50 years.

Wood Siding

Classic natural beauty. Cedar and other species available. Requires more maintenance but offers timeless appeal.

Siding Repair

Fix damaged, cracked, or warped siding panels. Replace sections to restore your home's protection and appearance.

Benefits of New Siding

Enhanced Curb Appeal

New siding dramatically improves your home's appearance and can increase property value significantly

Energy Efficiency

Modern siding provides additional insulation, reducing energy costs year-round

Low Maintenance

Vinyl and fiber cement siding require minimal upkeep - no painting or sealing

Weather Protection

Quality siding shields your home from rain, wind, and temperature extremes

Moisture Protection

Prevents water infiltration that can lead to mold, rot, and structural damage

Long-Lasting Value

Quality siding can last 30-50+ years, providing decades of protection and beauty
